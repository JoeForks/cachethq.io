What is {{ site.project_title }}?

{{ site.project_title }} is a simple to use and easy to manage StatusPage.io alternative and can be easily installed and used to track third party service letting your users know what's going on.

What does it Do?

  • Breakdown your service into components.
  • Log incidents manually.
  • Easy to use dashboard.
  • Implement your own integrations with the API.

How does it work?

{{ site.project_title }} is written in PHP, using the amazing Laravel 4.2 framework.
